Automatic Fish Detection and Tracking in Underwater Videos

  • Collected and labeled fish pictures manually to create a custom training dataset
  • Calculated the FPS to measure the performance
I wrote a step-by-step tutorial for employing Google Colab pro to re-train pre-trained Google Object Detection model for fish video detection (2022 ver.)
